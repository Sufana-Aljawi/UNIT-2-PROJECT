Overview

Tourism in Saudi Arabia is a growing industry, with the government investing heavily in its development. The country has a rich history and culture, as well as stunning natural beauty, making it an attractive destination for visitors from all over the world. Some of the most popular tourist destinations in Saudi Arabia include the holy cities of Mecca and Medina, the ancient city of Mada'in Saleh, the Red Sea coast, and the mountains of Asir. The government is also working to develop new tourist destinations, such as the NEOM megacity and the Red Sea Project.
