Makkah City

The Heart of Islam

Where the soul finds peace

Makkah is the holiest city in Islam, and it is home to the Kaaba, the cube-shaped structure that is believed to have been built by Abraham and Ishmael. Muslims from all over the world come to Makkah to perform the Hajj, the pilgrimage to the Kaaba. Makkah is also a popular destination for umrah, a smaller pilgrimage that can be performed at any time of year.

.About Makkah city

Mecca, the holy city in Saudi Arabia is believed to be the centre of the Islamic world. The journey to Mecca as the Hajj is popular to the Muslims all over the world but the Mecca travel is forbidden for non-Muslims. There are billions of people coming to Mecca for Hajj from all over the world. The Hajj in Mecca is the largest pilgrimage in the world. The Mecca city is Saudi Arabia is increasingly called Makkah. It is similar to the pronunciation of the Arabic word than does the English translation called Mecca. Non-Muslims are not permitted to enter the premises of Mecca, Saudi Arabia at any time of the year due to the fact that the Mecca travel and the Hajj are only intended for Muslims. It has happened in the past that several non-Muslims have entered Mecca; the penalties of it are quite harsh including deportation and jail time.

Tourism in Mecca involves some advance planning. There is a special visa required by every Muslim who wishes to make the pilgrimage to Mecca. There is a valid visa required to enter the Hajj grounds. In case of Saudi citizens, permission is necessary.
